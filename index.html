Crystal Collector

Instructions:

Here you will be granted a random number at the start of each round. This will be your target score.


There are 4 crystals displayed, each one has its own, hidden, random value. By clicking a crytal, you will add a specific amount to your total score.


You win the round by matching your total score to the random target score.

You lose the round if your total score goes over the target number.


Remeber:

  • The value of each crystal is RANDOM and will be hidden from you until you click it!
  • Everytime a new round begins, the value of each crystal will be different!
